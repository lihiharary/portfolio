Instructional Design 2025

Growing & Caring for Plants

Interactive Module for Children

About the Project

This interactive e-learning designed specifically for young children to introduce the three essential conditions for plant growth: soil, water, and sunlight.

The learning experience is intuitive and exploratory, allowing children to actively engage with the content rather than passively consume it. Through colorful animations and simple feedback mechanics, learners reveal each condition and advance to a knowledge-check quiz.

Design & Technology

All visual elements—illustrations, icons, and layout—were fully created in Adobe Illustrator, using a clean, playful, and age-appropriate vector style.

The module was built in Articulate Storyline 360, combining instructional design principles with custom visual storytelling.

Brand Identity 2025

Oreo Rebranding Project

Visual Identity & Packaging Design

About the Project

This project reimagines the classic Oreo cookie through a creative rebranding process inspired by storytelling and user experience principles.

The goal was to design a fresh visual identity that preserves Oreo’s iconic, playful character while giving it a more modern, cohesive, and emotionally engaging look.

Design Process

The entire concept was developed from scratch — from mood boards and color exploration to vector illustrations, composition, and packaging layout.

All visual elements were created in Adobe Illustrator, following brand and print standards for real-world packaging production, including front, back, and side panels with nutritional information and barcodes.

The project demonstrates a strong sense of visual strategy, brand storytelling, and attention to detail, emphasizing how design can connect emotion, nostalgia, and product experience.
